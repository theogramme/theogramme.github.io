Timeless Valley is a short walk simulator in which you take control of a humble worker whose job is the retrieval of items that are long gone.

 

Controls:

wasd - walking

left click - activate/deactivate the visor

right click - recomponse torn items

 

Find fragmented items with the visor. Upon retrieving one, you will receive a call. Retrieve all items to finish the game.
